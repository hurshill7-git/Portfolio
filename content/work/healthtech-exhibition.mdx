<Section eyebrow="01 — Context" title="From screen to show floor">

Health-tech conferences are loud, crowded, and fast. A booth has a few seconds to
say who you are and why to stop. The brief: take a digital product brand and make
it work at human scale on a busy floor.

</Section>

<Section eyebrow="02 — Approach" title="Legible at a distance, rewarding up close">

I designed the booth graphics, wayfinding, and screen content as one system — a
bold, readable headline that lands from across the hall, with layered detail that
rewards people who come closer.
